Haikyuu!!

Hinata Shoyo, who watched one of the volleyball matches, strives to become a “little giant” and joins the high school volleyball club. With difficulty assembling a diverse team, he must take part in a high school competition. There, their path intersects with Kageyama Tobio, nicknamed “the king’s playground.” And although his team suffered a humiliating defeat, Shouyou intends to continue playing volleyball and take revenge on Kageyama. And what will come of it, you will find out by reading the manga “Haikyuu!!”.
